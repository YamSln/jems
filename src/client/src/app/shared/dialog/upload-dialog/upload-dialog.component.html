Uploading Word Packs

You can upload your own word packs by clicking on the attachment icon located to the right of the word packs upload field

There are several limitations and rules when uploading your own words:

  • You can upload up to {{ _gameConstatnts.MAX_WORD_PACKS_COUNT }} word packs.
  • Word pack must be a {{ _gameConstatnts.SUPPORTED_FILE_EXT }} file, where the words are separated by a comma ( , ).
  • Word pack size cannot excced the size of {{ _gameConstatnts.MAX_WORD_PACK_SIZE_KB }}KB's each.
  • Each word pack must contain between {{ _gameConstatnts.MIN_WORDS_PER_PACK }} to {{ _gameConstatnts.MAX_WORDS_PER_PACK }} words.
  • Each word must contain at least {{ _gameConstatnts.MIN_WORD_LENGTH }} characters and {{ _gameConstatnts.MAX_WORD_LENGTH }} at most.
